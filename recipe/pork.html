Chinese Braised Pork

Chinese Braised Pork is a traditional Chinese food.

The taste is sweet and salty, mainly made of pork, green onion, garlic and crystal sugar.


Ingredients

  • 800g Pork+ 10g green onion+ 25g crystal sugar
  • 1tablespoon sugar+ 2tablespoon soy sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Put crystal sugar in the pot.
  2. Cut the pork into chunks and put it in the pot.
  3. Stir fry after adding soy sauce.
  4. Put in a pressure cooker.
  5. Add a pinch of sugar and green onions.
  6. Done after 5 hours of simmering in the pressure cooker.
