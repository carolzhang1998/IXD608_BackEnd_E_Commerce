Kimchi Fried Pork

Kimchi Fried Pork is a traditional Korean food.

The taste is sour and spicy, mainly made of pork, Kimchi, sugar, garlic and cooking wine.


Ingredients

  • 250g Pork+ 200g Kimchi+ 5cloves garlic
  • 1tablespoon sugar+ 2tablespoon cooking wine+ 1tablespoon soy sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Put the pork in a pan without oil, then add 2tablespoon cooking wine.
  2. Fry on medium heat until golden on both sides.
  3. Add garlic after frying out the oil and fat.
  4. Add Kimchi.
  5. Add 1tablespoon sugar.
  6. Add 1tablespoon soy sauce.
  7. Finish.
