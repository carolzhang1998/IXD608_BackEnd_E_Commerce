Chicken Drumsticks

Chicken Drumsticks is a traditional Chinese food.

The taste is salty and spicy, mainly made of chicken, onion, ginger, garlic and chili.


Ingredients

  • 400g chicken thighs+ 10g onion+ 10g garlic+ 10g ginger+ 10g chili
  • 2tablespoon oyster sauce+ 3tablespoon soy sauce

Cooking Steps

  1. Slice the onion, ginger, garlic and chili into the pot.
  2. Put the chicken thighs in the pot and pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce.
  3. Add an appropriate amount of warm water, bring to a boil, simmer over medium heat, and finally add salt.
